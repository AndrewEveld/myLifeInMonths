My Life in Months

 

Inspired by Isabella Benabaye, Sharla Gelfand, and Tim Urban, My Life in Months is a chart that can help you see the larger picture of your life. Each square represents one month, and each row captures one year.

Edit the table below to map out where you've lived, your career path, trials and tribulations, or other meaningful life events. Then, click Download to save your chart!

Warning! The download function will not work in the Facebook browser. Please open this page in either Safari or Chrome to be able to save your chart.
